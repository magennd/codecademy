Meet the Troupe!

Our team of elegant and obscure design and craftspeople are here to ensure your historically-inspired sartorial experience is.

Anton Bloom

Owner and lead designer of Bloomers, Anton Bloom has never been comfortable with comfort. After discovering the unique and deeply unpleasant sensations of scratchy pants during his time at a Georgian Orthodox seminary, Anton determined to bring his outmoded tastes in nether-fashion to the masses. He has devoted countless hours to studying undesirable underclothes in Moscow's Muzey Mody and has personally owned and worn dozens of items of underwear. This unique and impressive background makes him ideally placed to lead his crack team of weavers and seamsters, and to sweep aside the tyranny of decent underwear.

Jackie Runner

Jackie Runner's story is one of devotion to idiosyncratic sartorial choices. She is widely known in her native Northern Ireland for her stifling running clothes and scanty formalwear. She brings her singular Weltanschauung to Bloomers.

Justine and Louisa-Mae Pinkler

Renowned weaving and stitcher sororal duo, Justine and Louisa-Mae labour tirelessly in gruelling working conditions to craft the finest gossamer and the coarsest kersey for our undergarments. This dutiful duo work like literal dogs, barking and slavering about the place. Whatever their insane behaviour, their product is undeniably adequate.

Sam Mustard

Muscular Sam Mustard, who can bench 9kg, is our distribution maestro. He ensures our glorious products always make their way to their customers. 60% of the time, he delivers 100% of the time on time. You can't rush quality.

A message from our founder.

Studies would show that we spend more time in our underwear than we do in the bath and in the dentist's combined. I think this is incredible. However, just like the dentist's, no one ever said underwear should be comfortable. I'm inspired everyday by how people in the past achieved such elegance even while the world around them was miserable. I began to think that our problem in the modern world must be linked. I think it's true that, the more uncomfortable your underwear is, the more fulfilling your life will be. Suddenly the dentist's won't seem so grim. I love you all.
